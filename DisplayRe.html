For computer display repair, first diagnose the issue: check cables, connections, and power supply. Assess for physical damage, dead pixels, or backlight issues. Computer display repair involves troubleshooting hardware and software issues. Start by checking connections, cables, and power supply for physical damage. Diagnose problems such as dead pixels or backlight issues. Update display drivers and adjust settings to address software-related issues.
